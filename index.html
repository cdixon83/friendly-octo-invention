Peanut Butter Protein Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quick oats
  • 1/3 cup flax meal
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1/8 cup chopped nuts and or seeds
  • 1/8 cup mini chocolate chips

Optional yummies to add

  • chia seeds
  • coco powder
  • coconut

Instructions

  1. Mix all this shit together in a large bowl
  2. Roll into timbit sized balls
  3. Enjoy!
  4. Refrigerate in an airtight container if you have any left
